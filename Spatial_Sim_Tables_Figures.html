Summary of Spatial Validation Simulations

Daniel J Hocking

2/11/2016

I ran 200 simulations of the spatial model for the White River in Vermont with 359 nodes (~330 stream reaches) for 1 year (no temporal or spatiotemporal components). For each iteration, I varied the two parameters that defind the Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process and therefore the latent spatial correlation structure in the watershed. We used all combinations of \(/theta\) (0.5, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5) and \(/sigma_{ou}\) (0.2, 0.3, 0.4, 0.5). For each combination in each iteration, I fit the model including with and without spatial correlation.
